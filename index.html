Times as a Kid

The Beginning Hey let me tell you a story of how i was bullied for 5 years straight. When i was in first grade i was being bullied by Yarin and Leny. I was getting hurt by them close to every day in school but i didnt tell anyone. This was my biggest mistake yet; i decided to just let it go and it was like this for 3 whole years.
Finding an Ally Until something great happened: a new kid named Eitan joined our school. He was strong and he saw me being bullied and thats when he pulled a move. He helped me when i was being bullied and we were friends for a year.
The Struggle of Loyalty Then everything changed. A new kid named Ariel joined our school. He was very nice to me and only after 2 weeks we became best friends. We went to each others house at least 1 time a week. But after about a month, things started to break up. The bullies started being friends with him and he was scared he would also be bullied, so he left me. At this time the only kids I could trust were Nadav and Ori.
The Turning Point A year later, almost my whole class started to bully me—that included the girls and 50% of the boys. It was a very dark time where I felt like I had to hide who I was just to get through the day.
A Fresh Start Years later, I went to 7th grade and got into a sports class. This was a major change for me. Being in a new environment with a focus on sports and discipline helped me find a new level of confidence. I realized that my past didn't have to be my future. I started focusing on my interest in Minecraft and coding, building a world where I could be creative and safe. Now, at 15, I'm proud of how far I've come.
Moving Forward (25/2/2026) But now, on 25/2/2026, the same kid Eitan, who was there for me all that time, started to feel a bit off. He started hanging out with other kids and never with me anymore. I wasn't jealous, I swear, I just felt that our time was ending. So, I started being friends with a new kid who has been in my class since 1st grade. Eventually, Eitan dropped me; he started making not-funny jokes about me and didn't even care anymore. That's when I knew that time was done. It’s a new chapter now.
